About Us

ShopRocks is a platform for both lovers and non-lovers of exotic rocks to be able to buy and sell their one of a kind finds. Founded by the team, 3SE2IS, our goal is to provide more exposure to this niche market through this website. Our team are builders at heart and are constantly looking to improve the services provided by the website and hopefully expand its reach to multiple corners of the world.

How does ShopRocks operate?

ShopRocks operates similarly to many e-commerce websites where users who register through the website are able to enjoy putting up their one of a kind finds for sale. Users who register not as sellers but as buyers will also be able to enjoy member prvileges. All-in-all we, 3SE2IS are dedicated to provide you with the quality service and accessibility through this website.
